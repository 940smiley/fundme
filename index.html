About FundMe

FundMe is an AI-assisted platform designed to help people find financial assistance through various online communities and platforms. Our mission is to connect those in need with generous communities and assistance programs.

How It Works

  1. Search: Tell us what kind of assistance you need
  2. AI Matching: Our AI finds the most relevant communities and resources
  3. Connect: Get direct links and information about assistance opportunities
  4. Save: Keep track of your favorite resources for future reference

Safety Tips

  • Always verify the legitimacy of assistance programs
  • Never share personal financial information unnecessarily
  • Be cautious of scams and too-good-to-be-true offers
  • Read community rules before participating
